Overview
APPOS is a two-day conference designed to provide advanced practice providers (APPs) with the latest evidenced-based strategies to optimize care and outcomes for patients with cancer.
Attend this conference and learn the latest clinical and practical strategies to improve care for your oncology patients and communication within your healthcare team.
Learn
The latest practice improvements from health care professionals from across the country.
Engage
In discussions and interactive sessions about current issues facing APPs in oncology practice.
Network
With like-minded clinicians from your region and gain valuable professional connections.
Collaborate
In a peer-to-peer setting with other APPs and share your own experience.
